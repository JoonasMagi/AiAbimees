Manage Your Garden

Aedniku Abimees (Gardener's Assistant) helps you keep track of all your plants in one place. You can:

  • Add your plants with detailed information
  • Track planting dates and growing progress
  • Set reminders for watering and fertilizing
  • Monitor plant health and add notes
  • Estimate harvest times for vegetable gardens

Getting Started

To get started with Aedniku Abimees:

  1. Create an account or sign in
  2. Add your first plant with basic details
  3. Set up care reminders for watering and fertilizing
  4. Track your plant's growth over time

It's that simple to start managing your garden effectively!
